Green Building Fundamentals

Green buildings integrate sustainable design principles across site selection, water efficiency, energy performance, materials selection, and indoor environmental quality. Our platform provides comprehensive tools for planning, calculating, and implementing sustainable construction practices.

  • 📍 Site Selection: Consider proximity to transit, brownfield redevelopment, and environmental impact.
  • 💧 Water Efficiency: Reduce consumption through low-flow fixtures and rainwater harvesting.
  • ⚡ Energy Performance: Use renewable energy, insulation, and smart building systems.
  • ♻️ Materials: Choose sustainable, recycled, or locally-sourced materials.
  • 🏥 Indoor Quality: Ensure proper ventilation, lighting, and non-toxic materials.

Sustainable Materials Guide

Explore eco-friendly building materials with properties and applications

🌾 Bamboo

  • R-Value: 1.3 per inch
  • Durability: 20-40 years
  • Carbon Storage: 12 tons/hectare/year
  • Cost: $3-8 per sq ft
  • Use: Flooring, structural elements

🧱 Recycled Concrete

  • Strength: 3,000-4,000 PSI
  • Recycled Content: 20-70%
  • Carbon Savings: 65% vs new concrete
  • Cost: $0.80-1.50 per sq ft
  • Use: Foundations, flooring

🌲 FSC Certified Wood

  • R-Value: 1.25 per inch
  • Carbon Sequestration: Yes
  • Sustainability: Renewable
  • Cost: $2-6 per sq ft
  • Use: Framing, decking, finishes

🔬 Cork

  • R-Value: 3.6 per inch
  • Renewable: Harvested without felling
  • VOC Emissions: Very low
  • Cost: $4-8 per sq ft
  • Use: Insulation, flooring

🌐 Hempcrete

  • R-Value: 0.25-0.35 per inch
  • Breathability: High (prevents moisture)
  • Carbon Negative: Sequesters CO2
  • Cost: $15-20 per sq ft
  • Use: Non-load bearing walls

☀️ Solar Glass

  • Efficiency: 15-22%
  • Lifespan: 25-30 years
  • Energy Payback: 1-4 years
  • Cost: $2-3 per watt
  • Use: Windows, roofing
